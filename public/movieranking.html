The Best Movies According to Todd

My top 3 movies of all time.

It's A Wonderful Life

One of the most heart warming Christmas films with a great message. It also has beautiful scenery in the fictional town of Bedford Falls, NY.

12 Monkeys

A very interesting vision of the future from one of the most unique directors, Terry Gilliam. Inspiring story that there is always hope to change, either as an individual or a species.

The Truman Show

One of my favorite Jim Carey film where he excels at portraying a man maintaining his sanity and preserving his sense of reality. As his world is crumbling around him, Carey's Truman gives a stellar example of how to be brave in the face of adversity and take control of his own destiny.
